## Important Formulas (That You Probably Should Memorise)
- \\(\frac{d}{dx}(a)=0\\)
- \\(\frac{d}{dx}(x)=1\\)
- \\(\frac{d}{dx}(ax)=a\\)
- \\(\frac{d}{dx}(x^{n})=nx^{n-1}\\)
- \\(\frac{d}{dx}(f(x)+f(g))=\frac{d}{dx}(f(x))+\frac{d}{dx}(g(x))\\)
- \\(f`(x)=\lim \limits_{h \to 0} \frac{f(x+h)-f(x)}{h}\\)
